Interesting facts about Lima

  • Lima is considered the gastronomic capital of Peru, South America, and the world.
  • Lima has the highest number of people who speak Quechua.
  • Lima is Peru's most important economic center, with the country's largest number of government and business offices, banks, and industries.
  • Lima is the most visited city in South America.

Festivities in Lima

  • New Year: January 1st.
  • Pisco Sour Day: February 3rd.
  • Holy Thursday, Good Friday and Easter Sunday: March - April.
  • Corpus Christi: May 30th.
  • Day of the Peasant: June 24th.
  • Independence Day: July 28th and 29th.
  • Day of the Criolla Song in Lima: October 31th.
  • Christmas: December 24th and 25th.
